Welcome to IdleFanbase

I-DLE is a South Korean girl group with 5 members formed by Cube Entertainment in 2018. Known for their self-produced music and bold concepts, the group stands out in K-pop for having strong creative control over their sound and identity — led primarily by member Jeon Soyeon.

Members

Miyeon

Miyeon has a clear, elegant vocal tone that balances the group’s stronger concepts. She’s often described as graceful and calm, but also unexpectedly funny and slightly clumsy in variety shows. Her visuals are classic and refined, while her personality is warm and easygoing.

Strengths: Stable high notes, emotional vocals, MC skills
Vibe: Elegant princess with hidden goofiness

Minnie

Minnie brings a dreamy, airy vocal color that gives many (G)I-DLE songs their emotional depth. She has a strong artistic sense and participates in songwriting. She’s soft-spoken, thoughtful, and imaginative, but can also be surprisingly witty and mischievous.

Strengths: Unique vocal tone, composing, atmospheric concepts
Vibe: Dreamy, mysterious, artistic soul

Soyeon

The creative powerhouse of the group. Soyeon is known for her sharp rap tone, strong stage charisma, and fearless concepts. She writes and produces most of the group’s music, shaping (G)I-DLE’s identity. She’s confident, competitive, and extremely detail-oriented, often described as a perfectionist. Despite her fierce image, she’s playful and quirky off stage.

Strengths: Songwriting, producing, rap delivery, performance direction
Vibe: Charismatic, bold, artistic mastermind

Yuqi

Yuqi stands out with her deep, husky voice — rare in girl groups. She’s energetic, confident, and very variety-show friendly. Fluent in multiple languages, she’s often the mood-maker of the group. On stage she’s powerful; off stage she’s playful and loud.

Strengths: Distinct deep tone, stage presence, variety skills
Vibe: Charismatic sunshine with swag

Shuhua

Shuhua has grown significantly since debut, gaining confidence and stronger stage presence each comeback. She’s known for her sharp visuals and straightforward personality. She can be blunt but is very loyal and protective of the members.

Strengths: Visual presence, improved performance confidence
Vibe: Cool, honest, quietly affectionate
